MEDIAPIPE

At the core of the experience is AI-driven computer vision. Our system uses Google's Mediapipe library, a cutting-edge computer vision tool, to detect human body landmarks in real-time. When a user performs a pose, Mediapipe identifies key points such as shoulders, elbows, hips, and knees, and returns their coordinates. This creates a digital representation of the user's body, allowing for precise tracking and analysis of movement.

POSE ANALYSIS

Once the landmarks are detected, the Pose Analyzer takes over. It calculates angles between specific joints using simple math and trigonometry. These angles are essential for assessing how closely the user's pose matches the correct form, offering clear and objective feedback on posture and alignment.

SCORING

By comparing the calculated joint angles to reference poses, the system generates a personalized accuracy score. This score helps users immediately understand how well they performed the pose and highlights where adjustments might be needed. The result is a seamless AI-powered experience that guides users towards better, safer form every time they practice.
